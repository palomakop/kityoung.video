Live performance at Elastic Arts, Chicago. Kit Young, video. Allen Moore, sound. Cristal Sabbath, movement.

Black is Beautiful documents a live performance in which artists interact across mediums to create a work of interdisciplinary improvisation. With minimal editing and no after effects we are letting the original performance stand as it happened.
