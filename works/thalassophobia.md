Thalassophobia: the persistent and intense fear of deep bodies of water such as the sea, oceans, pools, or lakes.

TanukiSpiderCat — electric cello, pedals, modular audio synthesis, field recordings.
Kit Young — video synthesis, video feedback, cinema verite samples.

Thalassophobia is a live performance in which the artists interact across mediums to create a work of interdisciplinary improvisation.
